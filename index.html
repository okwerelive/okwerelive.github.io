Davey Prince

Davey Prince is an artist who refuses to be confined. A storyteller first, he weaves melody and lyricism into something larger than genre—a canvas where every word, every note, carries weight. Whether through raw confession or sharpened intensity, his music lives in the tension between vulnerability and defiance, beauty and chaos. Every line is deliberate, every emotion earned, every song a world of its own. For Davey, art isn’t about fitting in—it’s about saying what needs to be said, in whatever way it demands to be heard.

Push Kahlon is a multitalented artist with a strong foundation in rap, singing, and music production. His skills as a vocalist and producer allow him to create professional-quality tracks that showcase his unique voice and style. His dedication to honing his skills and continually improving as an artist is evident in everything he does, and his talent and drive make him a rising star in the music industry with the potential to achieve great success.
